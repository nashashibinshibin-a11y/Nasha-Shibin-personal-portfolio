About Me

Crafting Experiences.

I'm Nasha Shibin, a 22-year-old UI/UX and Brand Designer from Kerala, India. I craft digital experiences that live at the intersection of beauty and function — interfaces that don't just look good, but feel right to use.

Honed through an internship at Futura Labs and a full-time role at SpineCodes, my work is driven by curiosity, care for the user, and an obsession with the details that most people never notice — but always feel.
